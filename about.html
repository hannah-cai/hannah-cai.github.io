Hello!

My name is Hannah Cai.

I grew up in the Bay Area (specifically, in San Jose). Now, I'm a student in Carnegie Mellon University's School of Design, studying communication design. Things I'm passionate about include web design (I coded this website myself!), data visualization, art, coding, interaction design, audio/music, and motion. My goal as a designer is to create meaningful and insightful narratives that are both practical and aesthetically delightful.

While I'm mainly focusing on design in my studies, I also love taking opportunities to explore other things I'm interested in. Some of the coolest classes I've taken so far at CMU include computing for creative practices (p5.js), flameworking glass, intro to arduino, sign language, laser-cutting/3d printing, and intro to music technology (reason, pro tools, and finale).

When I'm not doing schoolwork, you can probably find me listening to music, drinking tea, reading webcomics, playing guitar or ukelele, daydreaming, working on a side project, or just lounging around.
