24-hour design challenge — job applicant tracker for recruiters

Adobe XD

A design challenge I received focusing on tracking applicants' progress in the hiring process. Design for two users and their respective needs: recruiters and interviewers. Decide which platform is most suitable (web, iOS, Android).

—

Debriefing

My first step was to analyze the design brief. I separated the two users and listed their requirements for the app, then took additional notes on how those requirements might manifest in design choices. Some things I wanted to include early on were color coding to show scores, as well as a simple way to show different stages.

user flow

I then mapped out user flows and continued to ideate on possible design choices. Something I wish I could've done here is some user testing on which design elements would've been most intuitive for recruiters to show the different stages/progress of applicants; I decided on color coded bars but I'm not sure if that would've made the most sense to users.

platform

While deciding on a platform, I tried to take into account both users' needs and the short timeframe for the exercise. I assumed that recruiters would probably benefit from having a mobile interface, so they could manage their applicants on the go; on the other hand, interviewers probably wanted a desktop platform, so that they could type notes directly into the system when interviewing. I decided to compromise and make an app that could work across mobile and desktop, but would remain at mobile size. I didn't think I would have time to design for both desktop and mobile, and also felt that a desktop-sized interface was unecessary and maybe even extraneous for simple note-taking.

sketching

future steps

This was a really short exercise, but if possible, I would've liked to do prototype the entire system in high-fi, and do user testing with it. A definite concern I had with this design was what it probably isn't very accessible for color-blind people; I would've liked to do user research to find a color scheme that's both accessible and makes sense connotationally.
